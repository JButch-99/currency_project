How this works?

Welcome! Thank you for using our program! To use this program, you will need to do a few things before you see your desired results. Below is a comprehensive list, numbered in order, for you to follow.

  • Step 1: Enter the amount of USD desired and click on "Submit Form".
  • Step 2: On the far left list of currencies, click on which you desire. Clicking this automatically primes it for conversion.
  • Step 3: Once both are chosen, click "See your results" to see the results.
  • Step 4: To use this program again, click "Reset Form" to choose new currencies.

NOTE: This is currently only for USD to another currency. Additional functionality will be added on a future date.
